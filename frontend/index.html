🤔 What is NDVI?

NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index) measures vegetation health using satellite imagery.

How it works:

  1. Satellite captures two bands:
    • 🔴 Red light (absorbed by healthy plants)
    • 🌈 Near-Infrared (reflected by healthy plants)
  2. Calculate NDVI:
    NDVI = (NIR - Red) / (NIR + Red)
  3. Interpret results:
    • -1.0 to 0.2: Bare soil, water, urban areas
    • 0.2 to 0.6: Sparse or stressed vegetation
    • 0.6 to 1.0: Healthy, dense vegetation
